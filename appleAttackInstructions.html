APPLE ATTACK

Collect apples to get rid of birds!

Use WASD to move around

Chicks take 2 apples to defeat and give you 1 point! A chick spawns every 4 apples you collect.

Ducks take 3 apples to defeat and give you 2 points! A duck spawns every 8 apples you collect.

Chickens take 4 apples to defeat and give you 3 points! A chicken spawns every 12 apples you collect.

If you don't have enough apples to defeat a bird, you die!
